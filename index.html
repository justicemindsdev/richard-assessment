SYSTEMIC ISSUES AT WHITE FERRY

Insistent Pay Pressure & Surveillance:

Reports indicate that staff at White Ferry are subjected to insistent pay pressure, which may contribute to a culture where critical issues are overlooked or deliberately ignored to cut costs. This is compounded by an environment of surveillance, where staff actions are monitored, potentially leading to selective targeting of individuals who attempt to uphold ethical standards or report misconduct.

Selective Targeting and Reporting of Vulnerable Adults:

There is evidence suggesting a disturbing pattern of selective targeting and inappropriate reporting of vulnerable adults. Instead of addressing the root causes of issues, the focus appears to shift to blaming or misrepresenting the actions of those who are either vulnerable or who attempt to intervene responsibly. This creates a dangerous precedent and a hostile environment for both guests and staff who prioritize safety and ethical conduct.
